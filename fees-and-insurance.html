Insurance

We are not providers on any insurance panels and do not accept insurance payments. We cannot enter into contracts with insurance companies. Unfortunately, most insurance companies do not cover the costs of psychological testing. We will provide a bill that can be submitted to the insurance carrier. Parents should not expect to be reimbursed for the cost of the evaluation by their insurance plan.

Fees

The cost of a neuropsychological evaluation is between $2400-2600, depending on the age of the child. This fee includes:

  • Parent interview
  • Testing of the child
  • A comprehensive report
  • A follow-up session with the parents to discuss the test findings

A deposit of $300 will be collected prior to scheduling the evaluation. The deposit will be applied to the total cost of the evaluation. This deposit is nonrefundable. We realize that unforeseen circumstances such as illness may arise. Should that occur then the evaluation will be rescheduled without the loss of the deposit.

No Surprise Act

The Federal No Surprises Act which went into effect on January 1, 2022, protects clients from "surprise billing." The law states that if an invoice is at least $400 higher than the good faith estimate, clients can dispute the invoice within 120 days.

More information about the No Surprises Act can be found at www.cms.gov/nosurprises.

Given that we bill testing at a flat rate, there will be no billing surprises.
