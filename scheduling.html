Initial Consultation and Parent Interview

The initial meeting will take place via teleconference. In this meeting, we will gather information about your concerns or issues you are facing with your child. We will review your child's developmental, medical, and educational histories.

Forms for Parents and Teachers

We will send you forms for you to complete about your child's behavior and we will also ask that your child's teacher complete rating scales regarding behavior in the classroom.

Parents can decline to have the child's teachers provide information about school behavior.

Testing Session

Our goal is to complete testing within one day as this mimics the school day and helps us understand how the child manages themselves and their cognitive stamina.

Follow Up Meeting with Parents

Within a few weeks of the testing session, we will review test findings with the parents. Prior to this meeting, forms from parents and teachers (if included) must be received by our office.

This session will be conducted via teleconferencing. Meetings can also be conducted in person if parents prefer. We will also review recommendations for home and school.
